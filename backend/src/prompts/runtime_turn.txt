# New Turn - Visual Analysis Required

You have received a new screenshot showing the current state of the browser.

## What to do:

1. **Analyze the screenshot visually** - What do you see? Where are the elements?

2. **Consider the user's goal** - What do they want to accomplish?

3. **Decide your next action** - Should you:
   - Find an element?
   - Click somewhere?
   - Type text?
   - Scroll?
   - Take a screenshot to see more?
   - Verify something?
   - Finish the task?

4. **Execute ONE tool** - Call only one tool per turn

5. **Wait for result** - The tool result will show what happened

## Remember:
- You see the screen visually - use visual descriptions
- Coordinates are normalized [0,1]
- One tool at a time
- Verify results with screenshots
